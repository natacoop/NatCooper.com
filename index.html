Nat in a Nutshell

Hi! I'm Nat Cooper, and I love building beautiful websites that promote great work. I am the Manager of Operations for LS Graphics Inc. and work from my home office in Toronto, Canada. I live with my partner, Jesse, and two cats - Moose and Izzy.

I am a student of the HackerYou Front-End Web Development Immersive, and I have an undergraduate degree in Technical Theatre Production from Ryerson University . I am passionate about helping artists promote their work online - through portfolio websites, social media, and resume design.

When I'm not busy building digital things, I'm usually playing board games or hunting down a delicious cup of coffee.
